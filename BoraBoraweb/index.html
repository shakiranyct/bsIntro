Home

Bora Bora is a small island located northwest of Tahiti in French Polynesia; their houses are built on the water expanding out from the island. The island is small but that does not stop them from using its beauty to get tourists. The island was originally created from volcanic activities. They are very proud of their homes on the island and would love to share it with others.
