People

Around 4000 people live on the island of Bora Bora. The main languages spoken are French and Tahitian. Bora Bora has developed a unique culinary tradition, they have infused the island with different traditions resulting in Bora Bora having a mixture of traditional South Pacific cooking with French, Japanese and Italian influence. Recently the people of Bora Bora have been trying to bring back more of its original Polynesian culture through traditional music and arts.
